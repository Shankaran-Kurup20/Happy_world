Mona Lisa

The Mona Lisa was painted by Leonardo da Vinci in Florence during the early 1500s. It is considered to be the most well-known work of art in the world.

Guernica

Guernica is an oil painting that was painted by Pablo Picasso in 1937. It is an anti-war painting that aimed to go against the bombing of the Spanish city as well as the fascist movments spreading across Europe.
